Emergency Go Bag Essentials

Water & Food

Always keep at least one liter of clean water and non-perishable food like canned goods or energy bars for quick nutrition during emergencies.

First Aid Kit

Pack medical basics such as bandages, cleansing wipes, pain relievers, and prescription medicines if needed.

Flashlight & Whistle

Remember to pack a flashlight for visibility incase there are blackouts. A whistle helps signal for help and draw attention when needed.

Extra Clothes

Pack a set of clean clothes, including socks and a jacket, to keep yourself warm and comfortable during evacuation.

Important Documents

Store copies of IDs, birth certificates, insurance, and emergency contacts in a waterproof pouch/container to protect them from damage.

Power Bank & Chargers

Keep a fully charged power bank and your phone charger to stay connected during emergencies or evacuations.

Stay prepared. Stay Alert. Stay safe.
